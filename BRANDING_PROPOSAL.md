## 🔧 QuickBooks Integration Strategy

### Recommended Approach:

**Option A: QuickBooks Online Integration** (RECOMMENDED)
- Use QuickBooks API for real-time sync
- Auto-create invoices in QB from ShiftForce
- Sync chart of accounts
- Export payroll journal entries
- **Cost**: QuickBooks API is free for certified apps

**Option B: CSV Export**
- Generate QB-compatible CSV files
- Import manually or via scheduled automation
- Lower dev cost, less real-time

**Option C: Zapier/Make.com Integration**
- No-code integration bridge
- Good for MVP testing
- Limited customization

### Priority Features:
1. ✅ Invoice sync (ShiftForce → QuickBooks)
2. ✅ Payroll journal entries export
3. ✅ Chart of accounts mapping
4. ✅ Customer/vendor sync
5. ⏳ Tax form generation (1099, W-2) - Phase 2

## 🧮 Automation Validation Requirements

### CRITICAL: Math & Calculation Testing

**Test Scenario Matrix:**

| Pay Frequency | Hours/Week | # Employees | # Clients | Test Duration |
|---------------|------------|-------------|-----------|---------------|
| Weekly | 40 | 10 | 10 | 4 weeks |
| Bi-weekly | 40 | 10 | 10 | 4 weeks |
| Semi-monthly | 40 | 10 | 10 | 2 months |
| Monthly | 40 | 10 | 10 | 2 months |
| Daily | 8 | 5 | 5 | 2 weeks |

**Validation Checks:**
- ✅ Time entry total = payroll calculation input
- ✅ Payroll gross pay = (hours × rate) + overtime premium
- ✅ Client invoice = (billable hours × client rate)
- ✅ Client invoice total ≥ payroll cost (margin check)
- ✅ Overtime calculations comply with FLSA
- ✅ Break deductions applied correctly
- ✅ PTO accrual matches policy
- ✅ Tax withholding calculations (if applicable)

**Liability Protection:**
1. Audit trail for all calculations
2. Calculation methodology documentation
3. Rounding rules clearly defined
4. Dispute resolution process
5. Professional liability insurance recommendation
