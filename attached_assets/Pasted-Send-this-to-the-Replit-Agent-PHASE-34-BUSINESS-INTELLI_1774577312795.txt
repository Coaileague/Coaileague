**STEP 7 — PRECOMPUTED ANALYTICS AGGREGATES**

Raw queries on large tables are too slow for real-time dashboards:

**Create `analytics_daily_snapshots` table:**
- Captures daily aggregated metrics per workspace: total shifts, hours worked, calloffs, revenue, payroll cost, active officer count, compliance violations
- Populated by a daily cron job running at 2 AM after payroll auto-close
- Used as the data source for all trend queries instead of raw tables
- Schema: workspace_id, snapshot_date, metric_name, metric_value, dimension (optional grouping like officer_id or client_id)

**Create `analytics_client_health_scores` table:**
- Daily client health score per workspace+client combination
- Composite score from: payment velocity (weight 30%), dispute rate (weight 20%), post coverage rate (weight 30%), support ticket volume (weight 20%)
- Populated by same daily cron job
- Used for churn risk detection and client health dashboard

**Index requirements:**
- `analytics_daily_snapshots`: index on (workspace_id, snapshot_date, metric_name)
- `analytics_client_health_scores`: index on (workspace_id, client_id, snapshot_date)
- Confirm existing indexes on: invoices (workspace_id, status, created_at), shifts (workspace_id, status, date), time_entries (workspace_id, employee_id, clock_in)
