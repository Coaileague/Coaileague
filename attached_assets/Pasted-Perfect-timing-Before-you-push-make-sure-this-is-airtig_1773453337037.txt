STEP 3 — ROLE MANAGEMENT AND LIVE RBAC SYNC

ROLE EDITING UI:
org_owner must be able to change any team member's role from their profile page.
The role change UI must:
Show the current role clearly.
Show a dropdown with available roles to change to.
Require a confirmation before applying — "Are you sure you want to change
[Name]'s role from Manager to co_owner? This will change their access immediately."
Apply the change to workspace_members.role immediately on confirmation.

ROLES ORG_OWNER CAN ASSIGN:
org_owner can assign any role below or equal to co_owner.
org_owner can assign: co_owner, manager, org_admin, employee.
org_owner cannot assign platform_staff roles — those are CoAIleague internal only.
co_owner has the same assignment permissions as org_owner.
manager cannot change anyone's role — read their current role, that is all.

LIVE RBAC SYNC — CRITICAL:
When a role is changed the following must happen immediately:
The workspace_members.role field is updated in the database.
A WebSocket event fires to any active session belonging to that user:
Event type: role_updated
Payload: new role, workspace_id, effective_immediately: true
The client receives this event and updates the session context without requiring logout.
The navigation items, page access, and Trinity mode all update immediately
based on the new role.
The user sees a notification: "Your role has been updated to [New Role] by [Org Owner Name]."
If the role was downgraded and the user is currently on a page they no longer
have access to they are redirected to their new role's landing page.
If the role was upgraded they gain access to new pages immediately.
This live sync must work even if the user is actively using the platform
when the role change happens.

ROLE CHANGE AUDIT TRAIL:
Every role change must be logged in the universal audit trail with:
Who made the change (actor_id and name).
Whose role was changed (target_user_id and name).
What the role was before and what it is now.
Timestamp.
Workspace ID.
This creates accountability for every role change ever made in the platform.
