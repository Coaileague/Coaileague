   - Track AI credit usage per org per billing cycle

   - Reset AI credits to tier allocation on billing cycle renewal

   - Check subscription tier + active add-ons before allowing access to features
   - If user tries to access feature not in their tier, show upgrade modal with clear pricing

5. OVERAGE BILLING
   - Calculate overages at end of billing period
   - Add as line items to invoice before charging
