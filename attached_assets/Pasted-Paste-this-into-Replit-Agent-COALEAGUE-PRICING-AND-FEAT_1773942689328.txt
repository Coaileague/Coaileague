SECTION 8 — FAQ
────────────────────────────────────────────

Accordion style. Gold arrow toggles.
Dark background.

Q: What exactly is a seat?
A: Every person in your organization counts
as one seat — owners, managers, supervisors,
and security officers alike. Your plan price
includes that number of seats. Trinity herself
is not a seat. She powers all of them.

Q: What happens if I go over my seat limit?
A: You can add officers at any time. Additional
seats above your plan's included count are billed
at your tier's per-seat rate, prorated from the
day you add them. You are never blocked from
adding staff — your team always comes first.

Q: What is an AI interaction?
A: Any Trinity chat exchange, autonomous task,
voice command, morning briefing, report, or
advanced analytics query counts as one interaction.
Scheduling, timekeeping, clock in/out, basic
HelpAI officer commands, and most database
operations do not count. Most clients use
less than 30% of their monthly allowance.

Q: What happens when I hit the hard cap?
A: Critical operations never stop — panic alerts,
incident reporting, compliance enforcement, and
officer HelpAI assistance always work regardless
of interaction count. Non-urgent autonomous tasks
are queued until your next billing period.
You are notified before overages accumulate.

Q: Do I need QuickBooks or Gusto for payroll?
A: No. CoAIleague processes payroll internally
on Professional plans and above. Direct deposit,
tax calculations, quarterly filings, and year-end
W2s and 1099s are all handled. If you prefer to
keep QuickBooks, a sync integration is available.

Q: How does the free trial work?
A: 14 days of full platform access including
Trinity's complete AI brain. Up to 10 seats.
No credit card required. No automatic charges.
At the end of your trial you choose a plan
or your account enters read-only mode.

Q: Can I change plans?
A: Yes. Upgrade immediately — new features
unlock the same day. Downgrades take effect
at your next billing cycle. Annual plans can
be upgraded at any time with prorated adjustment.

Q: What is an emergency event?
A: For Enterprise and Strategic clients, a
declared emergency event covers unlimited Trinity
usage for 24-72 hours during extraordinary
situations — major weather events, security
incidents requiring mass redeployment, or
regulatory audits requiring mass documentation.
Enterprise: $1,000 flat per event.
Strategic: $2,500 flat per event.

Q: What is Strategic pricing?
A: Strategic plans are for organizations with
300+ officers. Pricing is negotiated based on
officer count, states of operation, payroll
volume, and custom requirements. We respond
to all strategic inquiries within 4 business
hours with a personalized ROI analysis.

Q: Is there a long-term contract?
A: Monthly plans have no long-term commitment
and cancel any time. Annual plans are prepaid
for 12 months and include 2 free months.
Strategic plans require an annual contract
with custom terms.
