**SECTION 1 — CLIENT CREATION & BILLING RATE PERSISTENCE**

- Does editing a client and saving correctly PATCH only the changed fields — not overwrite unchanged fields with null?
- After saving a client edit, does the UI immediately reflect the updated rates without requiring a page refresh?
- Does `queryClient.invalidateQueries` fire after client create and client update to refresh all related queries?
- Are client rates stored in the `client_rates` table, the `clients` table, or both — and are both kept in sync?
- Is there a unique constraint preventing duplicate client records for the same workspace?
