**TEST 1 — CLOCK IN/OUT CHAIN**

Do this first because everything downstream depends on it.

Walk through exactly this sequence in Acme with a test officer account:
- Log in as a test employee on mobile
- Attempt clock in from outside the geofence — must reject
- Move inside geofence — clock in — verify time entry record created immediately in database with correct timestamp, correct officer ID, correct site ID

- Verify the entry appears correctly on the timesheet

**What you're looking for:** Any silent failure, any missing record, any wrong timestamp, any permission error, any mobile rendering issue.

**TEST 3 — TIMESHEET TO PAYROLL CHAIN**

This is the money chain. Test every step.
