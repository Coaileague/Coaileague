**STEP 2 — OFFICER ONBOARDING LIFECYCLE**

Officer activation must be complete and correct:
- Overage confirmation dialog confirmed — shows projected charge before activation completes (Phase 30 deferred item)
- Hard cap enforcement confirmed — blocks activation if org_owner has set a hard cap
- On activation: onboarding task list generated (Phase 48 scope — flag if not yet built), license record created or linked, onboarding documents generated and routed for signing, NDS notification to officer and supervisor, audit record written
- Pending officer cannot be assigned to shifts — confirmed blocked
- Pending officer cannot clock in — confirmed blocked
- Activation requires completed onboarding documents — configurable per workspace

**STEP 3 — OFFICER SUSPENSION**

Suspension is temporary — officer retains their record but cannot work:
- Suspension requires a reason — not a blank status change
- Suspension immediately removes officer from all future shift assignments — not just blocks new ones
- Open shifts created for any shifts the suspended officer was assigned to
- Suspended officer cannot clock in — confirmed blocked at clock-in gate
- Suspended officer's license records and pay history preserved
- Active shift assignments at time of suspension flagged and manager notified via NDS
- Suspension audit record written: officer_id, reason, suspended_by, timestamp
- Reactivation from suspension restores officer to active state — does not require full re-onboarding unless workspace requires it

---

**STEP 4 — OFFICER TERMINATION AND OFFBOARDING**

Termination is permanent — must trigger complete offboarding:
- Termination requires reason — voluntary resignation, involuntary termination, contract end, abandonment
- On termination:
  - All active shift assignments removed immediately — open shifts created
  - Access revoked immediately — session invalidated, portal access deactivated
  - Final timesheet period locked — supervisor notified to approve final hours
  - Final paycheck calculated and staged — cannot be processed until final hours approved
  - License records archived with termination date
  - All assigned documents retained — not deleted
  - Equipment return checklist generated if configured
  - NDS notification to officer (termination confirmation), supervisor, and manager
  - Audit record written with full termination details
- Terminated officer cannot be reassigned to shifts
- Terminated officer's pay history remains accessible to compliance officer
- Termination is reversible via rehire flow — not a permanent delete

---

**STEP 5 — OFFICER REHIRE FLOW**

Returning officers must be handled correctly:
- Rehire detected when an email address matches a previously terminated officer record
- Prior employment record surfaced to the hiring manager before activation
- Prior pay history, incidents, and compliance records visible to manager during rehire review
- License validity re-checked — expired licenses flagged before activation
- New onboarding documents generated if required by workspace policy
- Rehire audit record written linking to prior termination record
- Seat count updated immediately on rehire activation

**STEP 6 — CLIENT ONBOARDING LIFECYCLE**

New client setup must be guided and complete:
- Client record created with all required fields: company name, primary contact, billing contact, service address, contract rate, post configuration
- Initial contract generated from template and routed to client for signing
- Client portal provisioned — access link generated and delivered via NDS
- Billing activated on Stripe — first invoice schedule set based on contract start date
- Rate table configured — base rate per post, any differential rates
- First post created with GPS geofence configured
- Client onboarding task list generated — tracks completion of each setup step
- Client activated only after initial contract is executed — not before
- NDS notification to client on portal activation

---

**STEP 7 — CLIENT SUSPENSION**

Client suspension pauses service without terminating the relationship:
- Suspension requires reason and authorized approver (org_owner or org_admin)
- On suspension: all scheduled shifts for client's posts flagged, officers notified of temporary suspension, invoice generation paused, client portal access suspended
- Client's existing invoices and payment history preserved
- Suspension audit record written
- Reactivation restores all prior configuration — posts, rates, portal access

---

**STEP 8 — CLIENT OFFBOARDING**

Client departure must be handled completely:
- Offboarding initiated by org_owner with reason recorded
- On offboarding:
  - Final invoice generated for all unbilled confirmed hours
  - All outstanding invoices flagged for collection
  - Upcoming scheduled shifts cancelled — officers notified
  - Client portal access deactivated immediately
  - All client posts archived — not deleted
  - Contract marked as terminated with end date
  - Data export package generated — all client-related records exportable as CSV/PDF
  - QuickBooks final sync triggered — all pending records pushed
  - NDS notification to client confirming offboarding and final invoice
  - Audit record written with full offboarding details
- Offboarded client data retained per retention policy — not permanently deleted
- Client record remains accessible to compliance officer for audit purposes

**STEP 9 — DATA RETENTION AND EXPORT**

Every departing entity's data must be handled per policy:
- Officer data retention: employment records, pay stubs, compliance records, incident reports retained for minimum 7 years per Texas employment law
- Client data retention: contracts, invoices, service records retained for minimum 7 years
- Data export: departing workspace can request full data export before account closure
- Export format: structured JSON or CSV, all tables included
- Export delivery: secure download link with 48-hour expiry
- Deletion procedure: PII fields anonymized after retention period, audit records preserved with anonymized actor IDs
- Retention policy documented in replit.md as a permanent compliance truth
