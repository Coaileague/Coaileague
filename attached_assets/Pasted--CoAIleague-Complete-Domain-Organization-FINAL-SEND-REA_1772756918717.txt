## THE 13 DOMAINS — PERMANENT STRUCTURE

These 13 domains are the permanent architecture of CoAIleague. Every table, every schema file, every service file, every API route belongs to exactly one of these domains forever.

---

### DOMAIN 1 — `auth`
**Purpose:** Who is allowed in and what they can do.
**Owns:** Users, sessions, tokens, roles, permissions, 2FA, API keys, OAuth connections
**Tables include:**
`users`, `sessions`, `user_sessions`, `refresh_tokens`, `access_tokens`, `permissions`, `roles`, `role_permissions`, `user_roles`, `api_keys`, `oauth_tokens`, `password_resets`, `mfa_tokens`, `two_factor_auth`, `login_attempts`
**Service file:** `auth.service.ts`
**API routes:** `/api/auth/*`
**Future features that belong here:** Any new authentication method, SSO, biometric login

---

### DOMAIN 2 — `orgs`
**Purpose:** The organization as an entity — its structure, settings, and lifecycle.
**Owns:** Organizations, hierarchy, settings, onboarding, branding, invitations, feature flags
**Tables include:**
`organizations`, `org_settings`, `org_hierarchy`, `sub_organizations`, `org_branding`, `org_onboarding`, `onboarding_steps`, `onboarding_progress`, `org_invitations`, `org_members`, `org_feature_flags`, `org_registration_queue`, `mother_orgs`
**Service file:** `orgs.service.ts`
**API routes:** `/api/orgs/*`
**Future features that belong here:** New org settings, multi-org features, white-labeling

---

### DOMAIN 3 — `workforce`
**Purpose:** The people who work — their profiles, availability, and site assignments.
**Owns:** Employees, profiles, availability, preferences, certifications, emergency contacts
**Tables include:**
`employees`, `employee_profiles`, `employee_availability`, `employee_preferences`, `employee_assignments`, `employee_certifications`, `employee_documents`, `employee_settings`, `employee_status_history`, `contractors`, `guard_profiles`
**Service file:** `workforce.service.ts`
**API routes:** `/api/workforce/*`
**Future features that belong here:** Employee wellness tracking, performance reviews, new profile fields

---

### DOMAIN 4 — `scheduling`
**Purpose:** When and where people work — shift creation, templates, coverage, conflicts.
**Owns:** Schedules, shifts, templates, assignments, conflicts, coverage requirements, swaps, post orders
**Tables include:**
`schedules`, `shifts`, `shift_templates`, `shift_assignments`, `schedule_conflicts`, `coverage_requirements`, `shift_swaps`, `open_shifts`, `schedule_publish_history`, `scheduling_preferences`, `post_orders`, `schedule_templates`
**Service file:** `scheduling.service.ts`
**API routes:** `/api/scheduling/*`
**Future features that belong here:** AI schedule suggestions UI, shift bidding, new coverage rules

**API routes:** `/api/time/*`
**Future features that belong here:** Biometric clock-in, new GPS verification methods, break rule enforcement

---

### DOMAIN 6 — `payroll`
**Purpose:** Every dollar an employee earns — calculations, runs, deductions, state rules.
**Owns:** Pay periods, payroll runs, earnings, deductions, tax rules, exports, garnishments
**Tables include:**
`pay_periods`, `payroll_runs`, `payroll_line_items`, `earnings_records`, `deductions`, `overtime_calculations`, `payroll_settings`, `state_payroll_rules`, `payroll_exports`, `garnishments`, `payroll_audit_trail`
**Service file:** `payroll.service.ts`
**API routes:** `/api/payroll/*`
**⚠️ Special rule:** Do not consolidate aggressively. These tables have legal and compliance significance. When in doubt, keep them separate.
**Future features that belong here:** New state tax rules, tip tracking, commission calculations

---

### DOMAIN 7 — `billing`
**Purpose:** What organizations pay to use CoAIleague — plans, invoices, Stripe, usage.
**Owns:** Plans, subscriptions, invoices, payments, Stripe events, usage records, credits
**Tables include:**
`billing_plans`, `subscriptions`, `invoices`, `invoice_line_items`, `payments`, `payment_methods`, `billing_history`, `stripe_events`, `stripe_customers`, `usage_records`, `billing_settings`, `credits`, `billing_cycles`
**Service file:** `billing.service.ts`
**API routes:** `/api/billing/*`
**⚠️ Special rule:** Do not drop ANY billing table regardless of row count. Zero rows = no customers yet, not unused. Stripe event tables are a legal audit trail.
**Future features that belong here:** New pricing tiers, usage-based billing, credit system expansion

### DOMAIN 13 — `support`
**Purpose:** Platform-level support and system operations — the layer CoAIleague uses to manage the platform itself.
**Owns:** Support tickets, support agent assignments, system bots, system health monitoring, admin tools, platform-level announcements, escalation workflows, internal tooling
**Tables include:**
`support_tickets`, `support_threads`, `support_messages`, `support_agents`, `support_assignments`, `support_categories`, `support_escalations`, `system_bots`, `bot_configs`, `bot_assignments`, `system_health`, `platform_monitoring`, `admin_actions`, `platform_announcements`, `internal_alerts`, `feature_requests`, `system_maintenance`
**Service file:** `support.service.ts`
**API routes:** `/api/support/*`
**Key distinction:** This domain operates at the PLATFORM level (CoAIleague looking down at orgs). It is not org-level support. Org-level communications stay in `comms`. Platform-level admin operations live here.
**Future features that belong here:** New bot types, new support workflows, new admin tools, system health dashboards, Trinity bot management
