Awesome — here’s a plug-in, Replit-ready blueprint to turn AutoForce into your own Gusto (payroll) + QuickBooks (invoicing + accounting) without rebuilding what you’ve done. It’s designed to maximize platform profit (own the logic, rent only the cheapest rails).


---

0) Strategy: Own the Brain, Rent the Rails

Own: invoicing, payroll calc, general ledger (double-entry), reporting, customer portal, paystubs, PDFs, reminders.

Rent (cheap): ACH payouts/collections (Dwolla/Mercury/Relay), bank feeds (Plaid), optional e-file taxes (TaxBandits/Zeal).

Keep fees: platform fee on invoices + pennies-per-ACH cost.



---

1) Replit Monorepo Layout (copy/paste structure)

autoforce/
  services/
    api/                # REST + auth (FastAPI or Express)
    worker/             # jobs: payroll calc, invoice runs, GL posting
    webhooks/           # ACH/Plaid/e-file callbacks only
  packages/
    domain/             # shared business logic (rates, ledger, taxes)
    pdf/                # paystubs, invoices (ReportLab or pdfkit)
    ui/                 # shared UI components (if using SSR)
  infra/
    migrations/         # SQL migrations (Postgres)
    seeds/              # seed data (chart of accounts, taxes)
  apps/
    dashboard/          # React/Next UI (org/admin)
    portal/             # client portal (view invoices, payments)
  .replit
  replit.nix            # or Poetry/requirements.txt / package.json
  Procfile              # processes for Deployments
  .env.example

Procfile

api: uvicorn services.api.main:app --host 0.0.0.0 --port 8000
worker: python services/worker/runner.py
webhooks: uvicorn services.webhooks.main:app --host 0.0.0.0 --port 7000

3) Minimum New Modules You Need

A) General Ledger (GL) — your QuickBooks core

Tables: accounts (CoA), journals, journal_lines, bank_connections, bank_tx, reconciliations.

Features:

Double-entry posting for every financial event:

Invoice send: Dr A/R, Cr Revenue

Payment received (ACH): Dr Cash, Cr A/R

Payroll gross: Dr Payroll Expense, Cr Wages Payable

Payroll taxes: Dr Payroll Tax Expense, Cr Tax Payable

Employee net payout: Dr Wages Payable, Cr Cash


Reconciliation: match Plaid bank transactions → journals (tolerance rules).

Chart of Accounts seed (assets, liabilities, equity, revenue, expense).


APIs:

POST /gl/journal (atomic post)

GET /gl/trial-balance?as_of=...

POST /gl/reconcile (attach bank_tx → journal_id)



B) AR & Invoicing — your QB Invoicing

You already have invoicing; add:

Invoice numbering, templates, multi-site line items, credits/discounts.

Collections: ACH pull via Dw
