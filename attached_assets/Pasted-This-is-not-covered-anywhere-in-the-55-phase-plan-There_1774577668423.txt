**TRINITY AUTONOMOUS SALES FLOW:**

**Stage 1 — Lead Capture:**
- Manual entry by manager/owner via sales pipeline UI
- Inbound inquiry via support@ or a dedicated sales@ email address
- Trinity parses inbound inquiries and automatically creates a lead record
- Lead score calculated immediately: company size signals, urgency language, geographic fit, post type match

**Stage 2 — Qualification:**
- Trinity reviews the lead data and scores it 0-100
- High score (70+): Trinity recommends immediate outreach, drafts first email for org_owner review
- Medium score (40-69): Trinity flags for manual qualification review
- Low score (below 40): Trinity marks as low priority, suggests nurture sequence
- Org_owner approves or modifies Trinity's recommendation before any outreach

**Stage 3 — Outreach Active:**
- Trinity initiates a personalized outreach email sequence
- Email 1: Introduction and value proposition — sent from org_owner's email address via NDS
- Email 2 (3 days later if no response): Follow-up with a specific differentiator relevant to the prospect's industry or location
- Email 3 (7 days later if no response): Final outreach with a specific offer or call-to-action
- All emails personalized using lead data — company name, contact name, location, post type needs
- Prospect responses received via inbound email routing — Trinity reads and classifies the response
- Positive response: Trinity drafts a reply and flags for org_owner review before sending
- Request for proposal: Trinity automatically advances to Stage 4
- Not interested: Trinity marks lead as lost with reason, moves to nurture list
- No response after 3 emails: Trinity marks as cold, schedules re-engagement in 30 days

**Stage 4 — Proposal Generation:**
- Trinity gathers required information: estimated officer count, post locations, coverage schedule, contract term, requested services
- If information is incomplete Trinity asks the prospect via email to provide missing details
- Trinity generates a professional proposal document using the workspace's proposal template
- Proposal includes: executive summary, scope of services, staffing plan, pricing breakdown, terms and conditions, org_owner signature block, prospect signature block
- Proposal PDF generated with SHA-256 hash, stored in document vault
- Org_owner receives NDS notification: "Trinity has generated a proposal for [Company Name] — review and approve before sending"
- Org_owner reviews in portal, can edit pricing or scope, then approves
- On org_owner approval: proposal digitally signed by org_owner, sent to prospect via NDS with secure signing link

**Stage 5 — Proposal Approval:**
- Prospect receives proposal via email with secure link to view and sign
- Prospect signs in the portal signing flow (Phase 18 infrastructure)
- Proposal status transitions: sent → viewed → approved or rejected
- On approval: Trinity immediately advances to contract generation
- On rejection: Trinity sends a follow-up asking for feedback, logs the reason, marks stage appropriately

**Stage 6 — Contract Generation and Execution:**
- Trinity generates the service contract from the approved proposal data
- Contract includes all commercial terms from the proposal plus standard legal terms
- Same signing flow as proposal — org_owner signs first, then prospect
- On full execution: contract stored in document vault with SHA-256 hash
- Client record automatically created in CoAIleague using prospect data
- Billing activated on Stripe with contract terms
- Client portal provisioned and access link sent to client contact

**Stage 7 — Client Onboarding Handoff:**
- Trinity sends a welcome email to the new client introducing their dedicated contact
- Trinity initiates the data gathering flow for operations setup:
  - Sends a structured questionnaire to the client: post addresses, entry procedures, emergency contacts, post-specific instructions, parking and access information, key holder contacts
  - Receives completed questionnaire responses
  - Creates post records in CoAIleague from questionnaire data
  - Configures GPS geofences for each post
  - Generates post order documents from the provided information
  - Creates the initial schedule template based on contracted coverage
- Org_owner receives a handoff summary: new client profile complete, posts configured, first invoice scheduled
- Lead marked as `onboarded` — sales cycle complete
