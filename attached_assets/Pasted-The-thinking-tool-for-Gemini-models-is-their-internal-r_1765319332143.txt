The "thinking tool" for Gemini models is their internal reasoning process, which you can control and expose through specific API parameters.This capability is central to making your Trinity agents smarter than simple function callers. You need to tell Replit to integrate these specific parameters and tools:🛠️ Essential Gemini Tools and ParametersThe tools fall into two main categories: Internal Reasoning Controls (telling Gemini how to think) and Tool Use Controls (telling Gemini how to use your 93 actions).1.
