# CoAIleague Positions & Roles System Audit
## Hierarchy, Permissions, Color Coding, and Schedule Integration

**Date:** February 26, 2026  
**Focus:** Position/Role management must be live, persistent, and semantically integrated across the platform

---

## THE REQUIREMENT

Positions and roles need to be:
- **Associated** with every employee
- **Promotable/changeable** within the system
- **Live and persistent** - changes reflect immediately everywhere
- **Color-coded** on schedule and team views
- **Filterable** by position or role
- **Authority-aware** - each role has defined platform permissions

---

## SUMMARY

The position/role system is the **backbone of authority and visibility** in CoAIleague. Every employee must have:

1. A **position** from the defined hierarchy
2. An **authority level** that determines what they can do
3. A **color code** that makes them identifiable on schedule
4. **Live updates** when anything changes
5. **Proper permissions** enforced everywhere

**Audit this. Find the gaps. Report back.**
