## The Storage Question — Replit's 100GB Is Not the Constraint

The 100GB Replit limit is for **your app code and database**, not tenant files. All file storage already routes to **Google Cloud Storage (GCS)** — your audit confirmed `uploadFileToObjectStorage()` is the write path. GCS is billed separately and scales to petabytes. Replit's disk never sees a tenant's photo, email attachment, or PDF.

So the real question is: what do you charge tenants for, and when do you cut them off?

## The Agent's Recommendation Was Correct — Go With Option B
