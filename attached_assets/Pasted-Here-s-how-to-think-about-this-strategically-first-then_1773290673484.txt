**Bucket 1 — Must Work Perfectly Or Don't Launch**

**Bucket 2 — Must Work Well Enough**

**BUCKET 1 — ZERO TOLERANCE FEATURES**

These are non-negotiable. Every single one must be airtight before Statewide goes live.

---

**1. CLOCK IN / CLOCK OUT**
This is the heartbeat of everything. If this breaks nothing else works.

- GPS geofence enforcing correctly — officer cannot clock in from home
- Clock in creates a time entry record immediately — no delay, no silent failure
- Clock out closes the record with correct end timestamp
- Overnight shift boundary handled — shift crossing midnight calculates correctly
- Duplicate clock in prevention — officer already clocked in cannot clock in again
- If GPS signal lost — graceful fallback with flagged entry for supervisor review, never a silent failure
- Clock records visible to officer immediately after action
- Clock records visible to manager in real time
- Manual override by supervisor — logged with who overrode, why, and when
- All timestamps stored UTC, displayed in correct local timezone
- Mobile clock in verified on both iOS and Android browsers

---

**2. SCHEDULE SYSTEM**
Officers need to know where to be. Managers need to know who is where.

- Shift creation by manager — start time, end time, site, officer assigned
- Shift visible to assigned officer on their schedule view immediately after publish
- Schedule visible on mobile without distortion
- Shift conflict detection — same officer cannot be scheduled for overlapping shifts
- Unassigned shift visibility — manager sees open shifts needing coverage
- Schedule publish triggers notification to affected officers
- Officer can view their upcoming schedule minimum 2 weeks forward
- Past schedule history preserved and viewable
- Shift cancellation notifies officer immediately
- Schedule data feeds clock in — officer clocking in outside a scheduled shift is flagged not blocked

---

**3. TIMESHEET SYSTEM**
This is the bridge between clock records and money. It must be mathematically perfect.

- Timesheet auto-generates from clock in/out records — zero manual data entry required
- Hours calculated correctly for every entry including overnight shifts
- Overtime calculated correctly — verify which overtime rule applies
- Timesheet period matches pay period exactly — no gaps, no overlaps
- Manager timesheet review view — all entries for their team in one place
- Approve individual entries and bulk approve
- Deny with required reason — denied entry routes back to employee with reason
- Employee can see their timesheet status — pending, approved, denied
- Approved timesheets lock — cannot be edited without supervisor override with audit log
- Timesheet export — generates report usable for payroll processing
- Any clock record discrepancy flagged on timesheet — missing clock out, unusual hours, GPS mismatch

---

**4. PAYROLL PIPELINE**
Real money. Zero tolerance for errors.

- Approved timesheet hours flow directly into payroll calculation — no manual re-entry
- Correct pay rate applied per employee — supports different rates per employee
- Overtime rate applied correctly where applicable
- Pay period totals calculated and displayed before approval
- Payroll approval workflow — org owner or authorized approver must verify before anything processes
- Trinity advisory on payroll — surfaces anomalies before approval, never processes automatically
- 1% human intervention enforced — Trinity prepares, human approves, system executes
- Payroll record created and locked after approval — immutable audit trail
- Employee pay stub generated — accessible to employee after payroll runs
- If external payroll processor used — export format matches exactly what they require
- Failed or rejected payroll — graceful handling with clear error, no partial processing
- Payroll history preserved — every pay period accessible indefinitely

---

**5. CLIENT BILLING PIPELINE**
This is revenue. Security company gets paid by their clients based on what CoAIleague tracks.

- Client billing rates configured per client — different clients billed differently
- Billable hours calculated from approved timesheets — approved hours become billable hours
- Invoice generated with correct line items — dates, hours, rate, total
- Invoice reflects correct client branding if white labeled
- Invoice delivered to client via email through Resend pipeline
- Online payment portal functional — client can pay invoice directly
- Payment confirmation updates invoice status to paid immediately
- Partial payment handling — invoice shows balance remaining
- Overdue invoice flagging — Trinity alerts org owner of unpaid invoices past due date
- Invoice history preserved — all invoices accessible with status
- Revenue reporting — org owner can see total billed, total collected, outstanding balance

---

**6. MONEY MOVEMENT AUDIT TRAIL**
Every dollar that touches this platform must have an immutable record.

- Every timesheet approval logged — who approved, when, what period
- Every payroll run logged — who authorized, when, what amount, what period
- Every invoice generated logged — client, amount, date, status
- Every payment received logged — amount, date, invoice reference
- Every credit deduction logged — what action, how many credits, timestamp
- No record in any of these categories can be edited or deleted — only appended with correction notes
- Full audit log exportable by org owner at any time
