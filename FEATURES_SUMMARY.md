  - Total workspaces
  - Monthly revenue

- **System Status**:
  - Uptime tracker
  - Database health indicator
  - API status monitoring
- **Quick Actions**: Direct links to Support and Usage dashboards

**Marketing Value**: Enterprise-grade monitoring, real-time insights, professional ops dashboard

---

### 4. ✅ Employee Portal
**Location**: `/employee/portal`

**Features**:
- **Personal Dashboard**:
  - Beautiful avatar with initials
  - Welcome message with role
  - 4 stat cards: Hours Worked, Earnings, Shifts, Profile Completion
- **Tabbed Interface**:
  1. **Overview Tab**: Personal info (email, phone, role, hourly rate), Performance summary
  2. **Schedule Tab**: This week's assigned shifts with time/date display
  3. **Time Entries Tab**: Complete clock-in/out history with hours and earnings
  4. **Documents Tab**: Employment contract, tax documents, payslips download
- **Modern UI**: Gradient borders, hover effects, professional cards
- **Data-Driven**: Calculates total hours, earnings automatically

**Marketing Value**: Employee self-service, reduces HR workload, professional portal experience
